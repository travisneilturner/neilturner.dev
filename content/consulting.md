I help engineering teams cut their Spark and Databricks costs and build data platforms that scale.

## What I do

**Spark Performance Optimization** — I analyze your physical query plans (not just your code) to find the anti-patterns that are actually costing you money: missed broadcast joins, unnecessary shuffles, scan inefficiencies, and join strategy problems. Every finding comes with an estimated savings so you know what to fix first.

**Data Pipeline Architecture** — Full platform assessments for teams running Spark, Kafka, Airflow, and Databricks. I deliver architecture documents with diagrams, prioritized optimization roadmaps, and cost reduction projections you can present to leadership.

**Event-Driven Systems Design** — Kafka architecture, streaming pipeline design, and event-driven platform modernization. I've built production event-driven systems at Riot Games (gaming analytics), StockX (marketplace), and May Mobility (autonomous vehicles).
